# AI 视频创作指南

## Three AI Creation Modes

Narrafiilm offers three distinct AI-powered video creation modes, each designed for different content types and creative workflows.

---

## 1. 🎙️ AI Video Commentary

Transform raw videos into engaging narrated content with AI-generated scripts and voiceovers.

### Workflow

```
Import Video → Scene Analysis → Script Generation → Voice Synthesis → Subtitle Creation → Export
```

### Features

- **Scene Understanding**: Automatically detects and segments video scenes
- **Script Generation**: Creates engaging commentary based on video content
- **Voice Synthesis**: Natural AI voiceovers with multiple voice options
- **Dynamic Subtitles**: Styled subtitles synced to narration

### Best For

- Tutorial videos
- Product reviews
- Educational content
- News commentary

---

## 2. 🎵 AI Video Mashup

Create seamless video mashups by combining multiple clips with intelligent beat detection and automatic transitions.

### Workflow

```
Import Multiple Clips → BPM Analysis → Beat Matching → Smart Editing → Transition Synthesis → Export
```

### Features

- **BPM Detection**: Automatically detects tempo in music/tracks
- **Beat Synchronization**: Aligns video cuts to music beats
- **Smart Transitions**: AI-generated transitions between clips
- **Multi-Track Timeline**: Full timeline control

### Best For

- Music videos
- Highlight reels
- Sports recaps
- Event montages

---

## 3. 🎭 AI First-Person Monologue

Generate emotional first-person narratives with cinematic subtitles from video content.

### Workflow

```
Import Video → Emotion Analysis → Monologue Generation → Voice Synthesis → Cinematic Subtitles → Export
```

### Features

- **Emotion Detection**: Analyzes facial expressions and tone
- **Monologue Writing**: Generates compelling first-person narratives
- **Emotional Voice**: Custom voice synthesis for emotional delivery
- **Cinematic Subtitles**: Professional subtitle styling

### Best For

- Personal stories
- Travel vlogs
- Documentaries
- Personal brand content

---

## Choosing the Right Mode

| Your Goal | Recommended Mode |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Explain or teach something | AI Commentary |
| Create rhythmic, music-driven content | AI Mashup |
| Tell a personal or emotional story | AI Monologue |

## Tips for Best Results

1. **High-Quality Source**: Start with clear, well-lit video footage
2. **Clear Audio**: Good audio quality improves AI analysis
3. **Specific Prompts**: When customizing scripts, be specific about tone and style
4. **Review Before Export**: Always preview AI-generated content before final export
